About Us

Welcome to the Skeptical Inquiry Network (SIN), a platform dedicated to promoting critical thinking and scientific inquiry. Our mission is to foster a community that encourages skepticism and evidence-based reasoning, empowering individuals to question assumptions and seek the truth.

SIN was incorporated as a non-profit making association under Section 17 of the Eswatini Companies Act No. 8 of 2009 on 13 June 2024. We strive to create a safe space for dialogue, education, and collaboration, where members can share knowledge and resources to enhance public understanding of science and skepticism.

Join us as we work towards a more informed society, advocating for transparency, accountability, and the application of scientific principles in everyday life.

Membership

Joining the Skeptical Inquiry Network (SIN) provides you with exclusive access to a wealth of resources and opportunities to engage with a vibrant community of skeptics and critical thinkers. Our members are at the heart of our mission, driving initiatives that promote scientific inquiry and skepticism.

As a member, you will benefit from:

  • Access to exclusive articles, research papers, and educational materials that cover a wide range of topics related to science and skepticism.
  • Participation in workshops, seminars, and events that foster discussion and collaboration among members.
  • Opportunities to contribute to community projects, helping to spread awareness and promote critical thinking in society.
  • Networking with like-minded individuals and professionals in the fields of science, education, and advocacy.

We invite you to join us in our mission to cultivate a culture of inquiry and rational thought. Together, we can make a difference in our communities and beyond.

Resources

The Skeptical Inquiry Network (SIN) is dedicated to providing a comprehensive collection of resources that promote scientific skepticism and critical thinking. Our resources are designed to empower individuals with knowledge and tools to question claims and make informed decisions.

In our resource library, you will find:

  • Articles and Research Papers: A curated selection of scholarly articles and papers covering various topics in science and skepticism.
  • Educational Materials: Resources aimed at enhancing understanding of scientific principles, the scientific method, and critical thinking skills.
  • Guides and Toolkits: Practical guides to help you navigate common misconceptions, pseudoscience, and critical analysis of claims.
  • Multimedia Content: Videos, podcasts, and webinars featuring experts discussing important topics in skepticism and science.

We are committed to continuously updating our resource library to ensure that our members and the public have access to the latest information and tools. Explore our resources to expand your understanding and join us in the pursuit of knowledge!
